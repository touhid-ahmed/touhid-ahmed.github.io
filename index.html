Touhid Ahmed

I am currently working as a Lab Instructor at the Department of Mathematics & Physics (DMP), at North South University. I am also an active researcher in the field of Autonomous Mobile Robotics. Previously, I have worked as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant (UGA) at the Department of Electrical & Computer Engineering (ECE).

The concept of robots simply intrigues me. During my Bachelors, I have worked as a Research student for Dr. Shahnewaz Siddique. I have also served as the Chair of IEEE North South University Power & Energy Society (PES) Student Branch Chapter which is the first PES student chapter in Bangladesh.

I am always curious to learn about new technologies, understand its fundamentals, and think of ways it could be implemented in my day to day life. Two of my favorite researchers in Robotics are Prof. Dr. Roland Siegwart and Prof. Hugh Durrant-Whyte.
